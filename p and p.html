Placement Procedure

Invitation

The Training & Placement Centre sends invitation to companies and organisations along with relevant information who are subsequently asked to fill the Job Announcement Form (JAF).

Slot Allocation

TPO and Recruiters coordinate to fix a convenient date for recruitment procedure viz. tests and interviews after considering factors like student preference, job profiles, etc.

Recruitment

The company may organise a Pre-Placement Talk (PPT) for eligible candidates and then proceed for taking the test on any platform which may comprise of multiple rounds as per the requirement of recruiters.

Results

The company is required to furnish the final list of selected students at the end of the assigned interview slot.
